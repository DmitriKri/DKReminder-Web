Privacy Policy

App: DKReminder
Last updated: July 13, 2026

In brief

DKReminder is a reminder app that primarily works locally on your device. We do not provide user accounts or operate a DKReminder cloud service, and we do not sell personal data. The app does not include advertising or analytics SDKs.

Data processed by the app

Reminders, reminder settings, trigger history, and details you add to scenarios such as meetings and deadlines are stored in the app’s internal database on your device.

When you choose a participant through Android’s system contact picker, DKReminder reads only the display name of the contact you selected and adds it to the meeting details. The app does not request unrestricted access to your address book.

Data transmission and third-party services

DKReminder does not send your reminder data to developer-operated servers. The app does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party SDKs that collect reminder content or usage statistics.

Android backup and device transfer

Depending on your Android and Google Account backup settings, Android may include DKReminder data in a system backup or transfer it directly to another device. These operations are performed by Android’s backup and device-transfer services, not by a DKReminder server. You can manage system backup settings on your device.

Manual backup and restore

If you use DKReminder’s Export data feature, the app creates a backup file in the location you choose. You are responsible for storing, sharing, and deleting that file. When you import a backup, its data replaces the current data in the app.

Permissions and their purpose

  • Notifications — to show reminders at the scheduled time.
  • Alarms & reminders (exact alarms) — to trigger reminders at the selected time.
  • Full-screen alerts — to display a prominent reminder alert, including on the lock screen when permitted by Android.
  • Run after device restart — to restore scheduled reminders after the device restarts.
  • Foreground media playback — to play the selected reminder sound reliably while an alert is active.

These permissions are not used to collect personal data.

Storage and deletion

You can delete reminders and their associated details in the app. Uninstalling DKReminder removes its local data from the device. A system backup or a manual backup file may remain until you delete or replace it in the relevant Android, Google Account, or storage settings.

Children’s privacy

DKReminder is not designed to collect personal data from children and does not knowingly collect personal data through developer-operated servers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app’s features or data practices change. The current revision date is shown at the top of this page.
